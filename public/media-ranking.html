Best Pieces of Media - Movies, Songs and Games - According to Myself

My Top 3 Movies

Collateral

I've watched this movie several times and it never ceases to amaze me.

Interstellar

I'm a fan of sci-fi movies, but I think this one is one of, if no the best piece of media of this genre.

Schindler's List

I'm also a big fan of historical drama and/or war movies. This one is a masterpiece.

My Top 3 Songs

"The Red Carpet Day" by Versailles

A great song from a great band from Japan.

"When You Were Young" by The Killers

Few songs manage to invoke such strong feelings of nostalgia in me.

"Ao no Sumika - Where Our Blue Is" by Tatsuya Kitani

Same reason as above.

My Top 3 Games

Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty

An all time classic.

Dark Souls II: Scholar of the First Sin

Out of all Souls-Like games, this one definitely has a special place in my heart, probably for being the first one I've ever played.

Mega Man X4

I'm a big fan of old school games, especially sidescrollers and/or platformers. Out of all Mega Man games, X4 is the best one, in my personal opinion.
